Our story

Equinox Premium Car Rental Service® is a team of car enthusiasts determined to make your driving experience as enjoyable as possible. With our help, your dream of having a brand-new luxury sedan is finally going to become true. We take pride in every aspect of our business, from the minute you pick up the phone to the day we deliver your car. We believe service is the most important part of our business, so if you want a car we do not stock, we will endeavor to find the car you do want. Vehicles are never released without a thorough interior and exterior examination, full preparation and mechanical checks.
